Windward Sailing Club

I was a member of this sailing club in Newport Harbor and have rented numerous sailboats from them. They are a great service and all of their boats are very well maintained. My favorite is their Dufour 33' "Renaissance" - big enough for a couple on a long weekend, but small enough to easily single-hand.
