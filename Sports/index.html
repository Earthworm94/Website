Freshman Football at Katella High school  
Katella Freshman Football Maxpreps

The first year I coached at Katella we ran a Veer Option Offense and an offset 4-3 defense. The schemes were much too complicated for the level of player development that we were intaking.  We went 1-8-1. The next year we switched to a much simplier double wing offense and a 5-3 defense. We went 6-4. The following years the offense evolved into a Wing-T and then a shotgun Wing-T and the records continued to hold up. We won 3 Orange League titles, had an undefeated 10-0 season and never finished with a record worse than 7-3.

This experience still shapes my approach to instruction and leadership and the effectiveness of organization, planning, and how crucial it is to understand your students.

Myron Miller - Double Wing - the bible
Dennis Creehan - Delware Wing-T - the new testament
Ron Vanderlinden - Eagle & Stack Defense - great drills book
