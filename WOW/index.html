World of Warcraft

World of Warcraft is a massively multi-player on-line (MMO) video game that has 10, 25, or 40 person groups working together to follow in-game mechanics to progress. I became interested in this game when I led my first 10-person raid and really enjoyed the teamwork aspect of it. I lead raids on a regular basis and enjoy discussing the tactics and stratey of the game with others.
